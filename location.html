How To Find Us

Nestled in Nature

Easily accessible, yet perfectly secluded.

Our Agro Villa is located away from the hustle and bustle of city life, but maintains good road access. It's an easy drive from major airports and tourist hubs, making it an ideal retreat during your Sri Lankan tour.

By Car / Taxi

We provide accurate GPS coordinates upon booking. Secure parking is available on-site.

The nearest railway station is just 15 minutes away. We offer complimentary pick-up services if requested in advance.
