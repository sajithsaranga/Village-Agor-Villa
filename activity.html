Farm Experiences & Activities

What You Can Do

We offer hands-on agricultural experiences unlike any traditional hotel. Connect with land and nature.

Organic Farming & Planting

Learn traditional Sri Lankan farming techniques. Join our local farmers in preparing the soil and planting seeds in our organic plots.

Village Nature Walks

Take guided walks through lush green paddy fields, serene lakes, and rural villages. Experience true peace away from city life.

Harvest & Cook

Harvest your own fresh vegetables and join a traditional Sri Lankan cooking session. Enjoy the delicious meal you helped create!
